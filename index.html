Where Gurukul Meets Montessori

Rooted in Indian values, blossoming with modern pedagogy.

Little Gurukul Preschool blends the time-honored Gurukul principles of respect, discipline, and curiosity with the Montessori method of independence and experiential learning. Our environment fosters each child's natural development — sensorial, practical life, and cultural awareness — creating lifelong learners who are kind, confident, and creative.
